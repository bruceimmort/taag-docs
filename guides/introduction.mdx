## What is Taag?

Taag is a pricing platform that helps you implement affordable pricing. Mostly if you are trying to launch or develop a Saas product. Build while scaling!

![title](/imgs/landing-app.png)

## Orientation

When you first log into Taag, you will find a clean dashboard that takes you from creating a project to implement live pricing.

And here is what you can implement with Taag :

1. **Projects**
are the base of pricing. Each project gets its own pricing, subscriber base, and analytics.

2. **Pricing Plans**
within each project, you can create pricing plans according to the categories you have.

3. **Pricing Rules**
this is where you adjust your prices based on geographic regions. You can set prices for different countries and continents.

4. **Subscriber Management**
keep track of paying user of your service and where they are located.

5. **Analytics**
monitor how your pricing performs across the world. See views, conversion rates, revenue that help you refine your pricing strategy.
